My Dad's Hometown

Chebrolu is a village in West Godavari district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is located in Unguturu mandal of Eluru revenue division. The nearest town is Tadepalligudem.The AH45 which runs from NH16 in Kolkata to NH48 in Bangalore passes by the village with beautiful Godavari canal on the other side .

Education

There are 9 government primary schools, seven private primary schools, five government high schools, five private high schools, two government secondary schools, and two private secondary schools. There is a government arts/science degree college and a private vocational training school. Nearest engineering college and management colleges are located in Tadepalligudem. The nearest medical college, Divyangu Special School is in Eluru and Polytechnic college is in Tanuku. The nearest arbitrary educational center is in and around Ungutur.

Language

The official language of Chebrolu is Telugu

Transportation

Chebrolu has a railway station where passengers halt. Buses from Eluru, Dwaraka Tirumala and Tadepalligudem passes through the village.

Electricity

>

The village has a power supply for household needs. Electricity is supplied for 7 hours a day for agriculture and 18 hours for commercial purposes.
